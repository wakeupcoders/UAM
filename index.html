    About Us

    Unati Cooperative is a vibrant force that harmonizes science, nature, and the ethos of cooperation to foster a thriving and sustainable community.

    CONNECTING COMMUNITIES CRAFTING WELLNESS CULTIVATING COOPERATION

    The Unati Agri Allied and Marketing Multi State Cooperative Society Ltd. (UAMMCL) is the multistate chapter of UNATI Unati Cooperative Marketing-cum-processing society Ltd. a seasoned cooperative with 20 years of experience in mass livelihood generation, women empowerment and value addition of natural bio resources.

    • Doubling Farmer Income
    • Mass Livelihood Generation
    • Focus on Women Empowerment
